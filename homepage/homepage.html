Bringing grassroots together.

The community that is fighting for a federal basic income is diverse and wide-reaching. For years, the sharing of resources and tools, the planning of events, and community conversations about basic income articles, legislation, and more has been relegated to social media accounts or Slack and Discord channels that have a closed door design, requiring an invitation to access. These don’t allow for those curious about basic income to benefit from the amazing work and resources aggregated by the larger basic income community or for those already engaged in the community to find others across the globe to connect with.

This site, the Basic Income Community, is designed to be a fully public space for sharing, accessing, evaluating, and talking about everything that is happening in the fight for a federal universal basic income. Whether you are a seasoned organizer or reading your first article about UBI, the Basic Income Community is here to support you. It is lead by and designed for the basic income grassroots community. Welcome! Look forward to you joining the conversation!

Code of Conduct

The philosophy behind a universal basic income is based in the belief that our economy must be designed to work for the people. We are fighting for an economic system that places people’s dignity and freedom on equal footing with consumer spending and shareholders profits. We want an economy that recognizes the inherent value in each of us, beyond a paycheck and that addresses the economic and technological realities of the 21st century.

The Basic Income Community code of conduct is rooted in this philosophy and approach. Conversations here, and the language we use with each other, will reflect the care and respect we expect our economic systems to support. By joining this community, you agree to hold yourself and others accountable to participate in thoughtful discourse. Those violating this Code of Conduct will be flagged or ignored, whichever will be most effective in protecting the greater community.
